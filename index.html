/ 01About

A small team. A wide capability stack.

RAD Engineering is a Bay Area hardware product development and engineering consultancy. We design and deliver hardware systems — covering the full stack from mechanical and electrical design through firmware, sensor integration, and cloud connectivity.

Our team brings over 10 years of collective experience in new product introduction, taking concepts through prototyping, EVT/DVT, and into mass production. We partner with manufacturers and other engineering firms to bring new hardware to market.
